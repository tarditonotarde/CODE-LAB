As a UX and front-end designer with a marketing background, I developed Herta Security’s brand identity manual and scalable design systems, creating a cohesive visual identity across digital and print platforms.

Goals

  • Establish a cohesive brand identity: Create a unified visual language that aligns with Herta Security’s mission of trust, innovation, and technological expertise.
  • Ensure cross-platform consistency: Develop guidelines for both digital and print materials, including UI components and marketing assets.
  • Support marketing and communication strategies: Align brand identity with campaigns, social media, and promotional materials to enhance brand recognition.
  • Develop scalable design systems: Provide a framework for future product and interface development, ensuring efficiency and consistency.

Project Scope | Deliverables

  • Brand Identity Manual
  • Logo usage guidelines: Variations, spacing, and incorrect usage rules to maintain brand integrity.
  • Color palette and typography: Defined brand colors and typefaces for digital and print applications.
  • Visual identity elements: Icons, imagery, and graphical styles for marketing materials.
  • Marketing templates: Presentations, social media, advertisements, and corporate materials.
  • Design System
  • UI components and patterns: Buttons, forms, cards, and interactive elements.
  • Grid systems and spacing rules: Ensuring design consistency across screens.
  • Accessibility guidelines: Contrast, typography, and color usage for inclusivity.
  • Print Materials
  • Business cards, brochures, exhibition materials, and promotional assets.

Research and Strategy

To inform a strong branding strategy, I conducted extensive research on Herta’s industry, competitors, and target audience:

  • Brand Voice and Personality: Established a tone that conveys trust, security, and innovation.
  • Competitive Analysis: Analyzed leading AI security firms to identify industry trends and differentiation opportunities.
  • Target Audience Definition: Defined expectations and preferences of customers, partners, and stakeholders.

Design Execution

  • Logo and Visual Elements
  • Refined Herta’s logo to ensure versatility across different media.
  • Defined proper logo placement and approved color variations (monochrome, full-color, inverted).
  • Color Palette and Typography
  • Selected a modern, professional palette reflecting technological expertise.
  • Chose readable typefaces with clear hierarchy for headings, subheadings, and body text.
  • Applied accessible contrast guidelines for enhanced readability.

  • Marketing and Brand Applications
  • Developed templates and assets for corporate presentations (PowerPoint, Google Slides).
  • Created branded social media graphics for LinkedIn, Twitter, and other platforms.
  • Designed brochures, banners, and digital advertisements aligned with brand aesthetics.
  • Defined website UI elements, including typography, color usage, and buttons.
  • Conceptualized and produced promotional videos to enhance brand storytelling.
  • Marketing Integration
  • Aligned all brand materials with marketing strategies to ensure a unified message.
  • Developed content strategies and customer-centric campaigns.
  • Collaborated with the marketing team for digital campaigns, trade shows, and corporate communications.

This project strengthened my expertise in branding, UX design, and marketing, allowing me to create a cohesive and scalable brand identity aligned with business goals. The Herta Security Brand Identity Manual now serves as a critical resource for maintaining consistency across all communications, reinforcing the company’s strong and recognizable presence in the AI security industry.

Working as a UX and visual designer in a startup environment gave me the opportunity to take on diverse design roles, from crafting brand visuals and marketing materials to producing videos and developing websites and apps. I was involved in the entire process—from initial visual concepts to functional code implementation—gaining hands-on experience that sharpened my adaptability, creativity, and problem-solving skills across multiple design disciplines.
