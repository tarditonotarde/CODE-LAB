Haters gonna hate

Haters is an interactive museum game where players can throw eggs, spray graffiti, and vandalize portraits of infamous figures from contemporary history. The experience is playful, mischievous, and encourages exploration, creativity, and a little rebellion.

Design Process

Conceptualization: Created a museum-themed interactive game that lets players engage with notorious figures through playful vandalism.

UX & Interaction Design: Designed responsive interactive portraits, drag-and-throw mechanics for eggs and paint, and intuitive feedback for player actions.

Visual Identity & Branding: Developed a visual style that blends museum elegance with chaotic, rebellious graffiti elements.

UI Components & Responsiveness: Built a grid layout for the museum walls that adapts to multiple screen sizes, allowing players to interact with each portrait seamlessly.

Micro-interactions: Portraits react dynamically to eggs, sprays, and other interactions, providing visual and sound feedback for playful engagement.
